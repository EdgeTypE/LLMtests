THE MAN

Mr. Sherlock Holmes

“My name is Sherlock Holmes. It is my business to know what other people do not know.”

— A Study in Scarlet, 1887

Born in the late 1850s, Sherlock Holmes is the world’s only consulting detective. He resides at 221B Baker Street with his loyal friend and biographer, Dr. John Watson. His powers of observation and deduction are unmatched. He plays the violin when he thinks, smokes a pipe (sometimes three at once), and keeps a messy collection of case notes and chemicals.

SPECIALTIES
  • 🔬 Forensic chemistry
  • 🧩 Cryptography & ciphers
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Trace evidence analysis
  • 📬 Handwriting & tobacco ash
METHODS

“When you have eliminated the impossible, whatever remains, however improbable, must be the truth.”

KNOWN WEAKNESSES

Boredom • Cocaine (in the stories) • Lack of cases
